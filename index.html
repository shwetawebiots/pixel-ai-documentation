Welcome to Pixel AI!

Pixel AI – Advanced AI Content & Image Generation Platform

Pixel AI is a powerful, enterprise-ready AI platform designed to empower content creators and businesses with cutting-edge generative technology. Built on a modular architecture using Next.js 16 and Node.js, it integrates seamlessly with industry-leading AI models to provide a unified workspace for text, image, code, and document processing.

Conversational AI

State-of-the-art chat profiles using GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini for nuanced dialogue.

Creative Suite

Generate high-fidelity images and professional-grade articles with advanced AI scripts.

Developer Tools

Integrated code assistant and AI detector to streamline development workflows.

Document Intelligence

Chat with your files (PDF, Docx, CSV) and extract key insights instantly.

Social Automation

Schedule and manage social media posts across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

Enterprise Control

Robust Admin Dashboard with User management, RBAC, and system-wide configurations.

This documentation is your complete guide to setting up, configuring, and scaling Pixel AI. Whether you are a developer looking to extend the platform or an administrator managing users, the following sections will provide step-by-step instructions and technical deep-dives.

External Chatbot Widget

Overview

The External Chatbot Widget allows you to create an AI-powered assistant and display it as a floating chat bubble on any website. Once deployed, your chatbot appears in the corner of your website and interacts with visitors in real time.

How It Works

  • Configure your chatbot settings.
  • Customize its design to match your brand.
  • Train it with your own data.
  • Embed the widget script into your website.

Once added, the chatbot appears as a floating support assistant on your site.

1. Configuration

In this step, you define how your chatbot works. You can:

  • Set the chatbot name and description
  • Choose the AI provider and model
  • Add your API key
  • Set welcome message
  • Adjust response behavior (temperature & max tokens)
  • Select interaction type

This section controls the chatbot’s intelligence and response behavior.

[Screenshot: Chatbot Configuration Panel]

2. Appearance

This step allows you to design your chatbot’s look and feel. You can:

  • Upload a chatbot avatar
  • Choose accent colors
  • Adjust corner rounding
  • Customize background and bubble colors
  • Modify text and button styles

This ensures the chatbot matches your brand identity.

[Screenshot: Chatbot Appearance Settings]

3. Training

In this section, you provide knowledge to your chatbot. You can train it using:

  • Website URLs
  • PDF documents
  • Custom text
  • Q&A pairs

This step improves response accuracy by giving the chatbot your own content.

[Screenshot: Chatbot Knowledge Base Training]

4. Embed

This is the final step. Here you:

  • Copy the generated embed code
  • Paste it before the closing </body> tag of your website

Once added, the chatbot appears as a floating widget and is ready to interact with visitors.

[Screenshot: Embed Code Integration]

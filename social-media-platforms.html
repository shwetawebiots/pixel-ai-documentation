Platforms & Configuration

Connectivity Infrastructure

To enable automated publishing and real-time analytics, Pixel AI must be synchronized with your social network accounts. This section details the platform-specific sync process and the underlying Social Connect Architecture.

1. Platform Management & Syncing

Individual networks are managed as "Nodes" within the Pixel AI ecosystem. You can manage multiple pages or profiles across Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

The Sync Process:
  • Facebook & Instagram: Synchronize your Meta-managed Business Pages and Professional Instagram accounts. Reach and follower metrics are updated in real-time once synced.
  • LinkedIn: Connect your personal profile or corporate pages to amplify your professional authority.
  • Sync Node Action: Click the "+ Sync Node" button on any platform card to initiate the OAuth handshake and select the specific pages you wish to manage.

[IMAGE: Platform management grid with Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn cards]

Infrastructure Security Protocols:
Nexus Integration

Secure Meta-cluster orchestration and cross-platform syncing.

Safe Protocol

End-to-end multi-layer auth systems and token encryption.

Rapid Revoke

Single-cycle authority revocation and session termination.

2. Social Connect Architecture (Configuration)

The **Social Configuration** page is where you provide the API credentials required to establish the bridge between Pixel AI and the social platforms.

Meta Platform (Facebook & Instagram)

Requires your Facebook Developer App credentials:

  • Meta App ID: The unique identifier for your registered application.
  • Meta App Secret: The confidential key used for server-to-server authentication.
LinkedIn & Real-time Integration
  • LinkedIn Platform: Enter your LinkedIn Client ID and Client Secret from the LinkedIn Developer Portal.
  • Ably Real-time Platform: Provide your Ably API Key to enable instant sync status updates and live metric broadcasting.
Security Awareness

Your credentials are encrypted at rest. Never share your App Secret or API Keys with unauthorized personnel or third-party services.

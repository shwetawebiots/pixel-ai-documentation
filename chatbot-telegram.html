Telegram Channel Integration

Overview

The Telegram Integration feature allows you to connect your Pixel-AI chatbot directly to Telegram. Once connected, users can interact with your AI assistant inside the Telegram app just like a normal chat.

This integration also works with the Human Support module, enabling smooth escalation from AI to a Human agent when needed.

What This Integration Enables

  • Users can message your AI bot through Telegram.
  • Conversations are processed through Pixel-AI.
  • Optional human takeover for live support.
  • Centralized conversation management inside your Human Agent dashboard.

Setup Instructions

Step 1: Generate a Telegram Bot Token

To begin, you’ll need to create a Telegram bot and obtain its API token.

  1. Open Telegram (mobile or desktop).
  2. Search for BotFather (Telegram’s official bot creation service).
  3. Start a conversation with BotFather.
  4. Send the command: /newbot
  5. Provide:
    • A display name for your bot
    • A unique username (must end in "bot")

After completion, Telegram will provide you with a Bot Token.

Store this token securely. It acts as your bot’s authentication key.

Step 2: Connect Telegram to Pixel-AI

Once you have your token:

  1. Log in to your Pixel-AI Admin Panel.
  2. Open the Chatbots section.
  3. Select an existing bot or create a new one.
  4. Go to the Channels configuration step.
  5. Choose Telegram.
  6. Enter:
    • Your Bot Username
    • The Bot Token provided by BotFather
  7. Click Save Changes.

Your Telegram bot is now connected to Pixel-AI.

Step 3: Test the Connection

  1. Open Telegram.
  2. Search for your newly created bot.
  3. Start a conversation.
  4. Send a message.

If configured correctly, your AI assistant will respond instantly.

If the Human Support feature is activated in Pixel-AI:

  • Telegram users can request live assistance.
  • The conversation is routed to your Human Support dashboard.
  • A team member can join and reply in real time.

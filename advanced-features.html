Pixel AI - Advanced Features

Scaling AI capabilities with automation and secure billing.

Billing & Subscriptions

Flexible monetization for your AI platform.

  • Multi-Gateway: Integrated Stripe, Razorpay, and PayPal.
  • Tiered Plans: Create Free, Pro, and Enterprise levels easily.
  • Token Limits: Enforce AI usage quotas based on subscription level.

Social Media Automation

Manage your presence from one dashboard.

  • Post Scheduling: Queue content for Instagram and LinkedIn.
  • Post Wizard: AI-assisted caption and hashtag generation.
  • Visual Calendar: Centralized view of all past and future posts.

Enterprise RBAC

Granular access control for teams.

  • Custom Roles: Define permissions for editors, viewers, and admins.
  • Departmental Billing: Split costs between different sub-organizations.
  • Audit Logs: Track every AI query and modification globally.

Custom Fine-Tuning

Train AI on your own datasets.

  • Dataset Preparation: UI for cleaning and labeling training data.
  • Private Models: Deploy unique versions of LLMs for your brand.
  • Knowledge Sync: Real-time RAG updates for your training sets.

Universal API Layer

Integrate Pixel AI into your existing apps.

  • RESTful Endpoints: Connect via robust API keys and secrets.
  • Webhooks: Receive real-time notifications for completed tasks.
  • Usage Analytics: Detailed monitoring of external API consumption.

White-Label Branding

Host Pixel AI under your own domain.

  • Theme Engine: Custom HSL colors and logo branding.
  • Custom Domains: CNAME support for personalized platform links.
  • Identity Provider: Use your own SSO/OIDC for user auth.
